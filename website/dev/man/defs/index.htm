Code Name Description
General
jdn Julian Day Number The internal day count date representation used by the Glich Hics extension. A minor variation of the "Julian Day".
jwn Julian Week Number A 7 day week (Monday start) count calendar, analogous to the day count jdn calendar scheme.
jwsn Julian Week (Sunday) Number A 7 day week (Sunday start) count calendar, alternative to jwn calendar scheme.
Julian
j Julian The simplest version of the Julian calendar, includes year zero. As used in astronomy.
jce Julian Common Era A version of the Julian calendar commonly used by historians. Year is marked as BCE or CE.
jn Julian Nativity A Julian calendar with the year change on the preceding 25th December.
ja Julian Annunciation Florence A Julian calendar with the year change on the 25th March, calculus florentinus.
jap Julian Annunciation Pisa A Julian calendar with the year change on the preceding 25th March, calculus pisanus.
eh Julian Era Hispanica A Julian calendar with the epoch of 1st January -37.
Gregorian
g Gregorian The Gregorian calendar.
gw Gregorian ISO Week A Gregorian calendar with year, week in year and day of week. Based on the ISO:8601 standard.
go Gregorian Ordinal A Gregorian calendar with year and day of year.
General Hybrid
ay Astronomical Year The Astronomical Year calendar.
French Republic
fr French Republic An astronomical calendar introduced after the French Revolution.
Hebrew
h Hebrew Hebrew Arithmetical calendar.
